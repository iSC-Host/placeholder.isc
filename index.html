Website Placeholder
A quick and simple image placeholder web service.

How does it work? Just put your image size after our URL and you'll get a placeholder.
Like this: http://isc-host.github.io/placeholder.isc/350x150
You can also use it in your code, like this:
<img src="http://isc-host.github.io/placeholder.isc/350x150">
